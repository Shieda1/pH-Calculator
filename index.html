What is the pH?
pH is a measurement of how acidic / basic water is, it has a range of 0-14 with pHs of less than 7 indicates an acid, 7 means that the pH is neutral and pHs of greater than 7 indicates a base. Simply the PH is a measurement of the relative amount of free hydrogen and hydroxy ions in the water.
How to calculate pH?
To calculate pH you should use the following formula:
pH = -log[H^+]
Why use pH?
The pH is used in order to control the availablity of nutrients, microbial activity, biological functions and the behavior of chemicals.
